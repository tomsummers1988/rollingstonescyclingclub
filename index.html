The Rolling Stones Cycle Club

The Rolling Stones are a group of likeminded medics, and the occasional non medic legend, looking to push the boundaries of indoor cycling

Training and breaking boundaries all year with a single goal in mind, the annual Tour de Zwift

For the 2025 event, Zwift have reduced the frequency of stages, the RSCC don't know less, they only understand more, harder, faster. So the format will change

Between the 6th of January and the 23rd of February the RSCC will be undertaking their biggest tour yet. 12 stages, the long and medium events each week - who will reign victorious
