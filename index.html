The Rolling Stones Cycle Club

The Rolling Stones are a group of likeminded medics, and the occasional non medica legend, looking to push the boundaries of indoor cycling

Training and breaking boundaries all year with a single goal in mind, the annual Tour de Zwift

For the 2025 event, Zwift have reduced the frequency of stages, the RSCC don't know less, they only understand more, harder, faster. So the format will change

Between the 6th of January and the 23rd of February the RSCC will be undertaking their biggest tour yet.

Either the normal tour - 12 stages, the A and B events each week, or for the ultimate challenge, 18 stages and the A, B and C races each week

Who will reign victorious

Learn more on Zwift.com

One shot per race, if you miss a round the slowest stage time plus 30 seconds is assigned

Take the risk, or lose the chance
